What makes us great? 

Chicle Base

We use a chicle gum base which is made from several Mesoamerican trees rather than the synthetic plastics other companies use.    

Variety of Flavors

We have 19 flavors to choose from. Ranging from our naturally grown mint and fruity flavors to our line of health focused flavors.  

No Artificial Flavors 

None of our products use artificial flavors or preservatives. Instead we use  natural flavors that still give our gum a wonderful taste but without harmful additives.

Sugar-Free

Our gum is made without sugar and instead uses xylitol as an alternative. This still provides a sweetening affect, just without the problems that regular sugar causes. 

Diet Approved 

Our gum is completely vegan and does not use any animal products. We also took into account other dietary restrictions and made our product, gluten, GMO, nut, peanut, and soy free.

Biodegradable packaging

In an attempt to be an environmentally friendly company, all of our products come in biodegradable packaging. Not only that, the gum itself is biodegradable making our product 100% environmentally friendly.
